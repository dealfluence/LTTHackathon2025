# Escalation Rules for Legal Bot "Lumen"
#
# If the user's query mentions any of the following topics or intents,
# the query MUST be escalated to a human lawyer.

# --- Keywords ---
- liability
- indemnity
- terminate contract
- sue
- litigation
- court
- unlimited liability
- breach
- data breach
- data residency
- non compete
- exclusivity

# --- Intents ---
- User is expressing extreme dissatisfaction or anger.
- User is asking for a definitive legal opinion or advice, not just information retrieval.
- User is questioning the validity or enforceability of the contract itself.
- User asks about a topic not covered in the provided documents.
